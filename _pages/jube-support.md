Jube is open source and free to use. Implementation and training engagements are fixed fee.
Ongoing support is billed at **$125 per hour** with no minimum commitment, and is a
genuinely good fit for cost-sensitive markets and clients with modest support needs. Hours
are available as needed, invoiced monthly, on a strictly best endeavours basis. Guaranteed
availability, callout coverage, and a retained relationship require an Enterprise arrangement.

For clients who prefer a retained relationship, Enterprise support is available at **$28,000
per year**, billed quarterly. This reflects a committed six hours per week at a retained rate
of $90 per hour, and includes:

- **Technical Account Management and Expert of Record** — all configuration, change, and
  deployment decisions are signed off and supervised by the Jube developer, minimising the
  risk of critical incidents before they occur
- **Regulatory compliance** — ongoing commitment to ensure Jube's tooling supports your
  compliance obligations as regulations evolve
- **Release advisory and support** — hands-on guidance and intervention through upgrades
  and infrastructure changes
- Weekly reviews
- 24/7 callout coverage on a best endeavours basis, with guaranteed response 06:00–18:00
  GMT, Monday to Friday

Support relationships begin once live transactions are processing and the installation is
stable, ensuring a solid foundation for a long term relationship.

*Ongoing support is available to clients who have completed Jube implementation and training.*

*All engagements are delivered under the [Jube Terms of Service (JTOS)](https://jube.io/agreements/jtos-version-1-5) — published, versioned, and written to be clear and equitable for both parties.*
