Jube is open source and free to use. Implementation and training engagements are fixed fee.
Ongoing support is billed at **$125 per hour**, prepaid in blocks of ten hours, and is a
genuinely good fit for cost-sensitive markets and clients with modest support needs. Support
is provided on a strictly best endeavours basis. Guaranteed availability, callout coverage,
and a retained relationship require an Enterprise arrangement.

For clients who prefer a retained relationship, Enterprise support is available at **$28,000
per year**. This reflects a committed six hours per week at a retained rate and includes:

- **Expert of Record** — infrastructure, change, and deployment decisions are signed off by
  the Jube developer, limited to areas that carry a material risk of service interruption
- **Regulatory compliance** — ongoing commitment to ensure Jube's tooling supports your
  compliance obligations as regulations evolve
- **Release advisory and support** — hands-on guidance and intervention through upgrades
  and infrastructure changes
- **24/7 callout coverage** on a best endeavours basis, with guaranteed response 06:00–18:00
  GMT, Monday to Friday

All support arrangements operate on a monthly billing cycle with a prepaid Hour Block model.
Unused hours are reconciled at month end. Enterprise clients benefit from discretionary
carryover where project cadence warrants it.

Support relationships begin once live transactions are processing and the installation is
stable, ensuring a solid foundation for a long-term relationship.

*Ongoing support is available to clients who have completed Jube training and implementation.*

*All engagements are delivered under the [Jube Terms of Service (JTOS)](https://jube.io/agreements/jtos-version-1-16) — published, versioned, and written to be clear and equitable for both parties.*
