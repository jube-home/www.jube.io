Jube is open source and free to use. Implementation and training engagements are fixed fee.
Ongoing support is billed at **$125 per hour**, prepaid in blocks of ten hours, and is a
genuinely good fit for cost-sensitive markets and clients with modest support needs. Support
is provided on a strictly best endeavours basis. Guaranteed availability, callout coverage,
and a retained relationship require an Enterprise arrangement.

For clients who prefer a retained relationship, Enterprise support is available at **$28,000
per year**. This is structured around a committed weekly engagement at a retained rate and
includes:

- **Expert of Record** — infrastructure, change, and deployment decisions are signed off by
  the Jube developer, limited to areas that carry a material risk of service interruption.
  Day-to-day use of documented platform functionality remains entirely at the Client's
  discretion and requires no sign-off.
- **Runbook maintenance** — a living operational document governing infrastructure and
  deployment, maintained by the Provider and updated following any material change to the
  environment.
- **Security patching and vulnerability management** — credible, documented vulnerabilities
  in the Jube platform are addressed through timely patches and releases, prioritised by
  severity and exploitability.
- **Regulatory feature mapping** — ongoing commitment to ensuring Jube's tooling supports
  evolving AML and fraud detection regulatory requirements, conducted on the basis of
  materials available in English.
- **Release advisory and support** — hands-on guidance and support through platform
  upgrades, dependency changes, and infrastructure modifications, delivered within the
  Runbook and sign-off framework.
- **Async-first support delivery** — day-to-day support is delivered via WhatsApp Business
  and Microsoft Teams, with screencasts and voice notes used in preference to scheduled
  calls. Meetings are available but treated as a last resort. Where something is urgent,
  WhatsApp is the only channel that guarantees an audible alert.
- **24/7 callout coverage** on a best endeavours basis, with guaranteed response 06:00–18:00
  GMT, Monday to Friday.

All support arrangements operate on a monthly billing cycle with a prepaid Hour Block model.
Unused hours are recognised as earned at month end — clients are encouraged to deposit only
what they anticipate needing in the period. Enterprise clients benefit from discretionary
carryover where project cadence warrants it, agreed in a monthly account review before
the period closes.

Support is available to clients who have completed Jube training and implementation and
hold a current certification.

*All engagements are delivered under the [Jube Terms of Service (JTOS)](/agreements/jtos-version-1-17) —
published, versioned, and written to be clear and equitable for both parties.*
