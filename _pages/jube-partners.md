Jube is open source under AGPLv3, with no per-client licence fee. For system integrators
and technology partners, this creates a straightforward opportunity: deliver a modern,
credible AML and fraud detection capability to clients who need transparency, control, and
regulatory defensibility — without the commercial friction of proprietary licensing.

Jube works within the part of the value chain where it adds most value: architecture,
development, and implementation of the platform. Partners hold the client relationship,
deliver the service wrapper, and build their own IP on top of Jube's extensibility framework.
Day-to-day platform use — rules, models, case management, configuration — is entirely at
the partner's and client's discretion. Jube's oversight is limited to infrastructure and
deployment decisions that carry a material risk of service interruption.

**What we offer**

For system integrators, technology consultancies, and regional delivery partners serving banks,
fintechs, and payment institutions in emerging and growth markets:

- **Training and certification** — the entry point to any partner engagement. Your team goes
  through the same Training and Integration programme as end clients, emerging with the skills
  and certification to deliver Jube independently.
- **Expert of Record** — Jube is available as named technical authority on partner-led
  engagements, supporting governance, audit, and regulatory documentation requirements.
- **Co-delivery** — for complex or heavily regulated implementations, Jube can participate
  as a specialist technical contributor alongside your team under a separate Work Order.
- **PAYG and Enterprise Support** — ongoing post-live support is available to certified end
  clients directly or through a partner structure, on the same published terms regardless
  of channel.
- **Async-first working** — support and co-delivery are delivered via WhatsApp Business and
  Microsoft Teams, with screencasts and voice notes preferred over scheduled calls.

**Pricing is published and fixed.** Training, implementation, and support fees are set out
on this site and do not vary by channel.

**Getting started**

Put your team through the [Jube Training and Integration programme](/jube-training).
Everything else follows from there.

*All engagements are delivered under the [Jube Terms of Service (JTOS)](/agreements/jtos-version-1-5) —
