Jube is open source under AGPLv3, with no per-client licence fee. For system integrators
and technology partners, this creates a genuine opportunity to deliver a modern, credible
AML and fraud detection capability to clients who need transparency, control, and regulatory
defensibility — without the commercial friction of proprietary licensing.

The Jube developers operate in the part of this value chain where they add most value:
architecture, development, and implementation. SIs hold the client relationship, deliver the
service, and build their own IP on top of Jube's extensibility framework. That alignment is
deliberate and worth something.

**What we offer partners**

- **Training and certification** — your team goes through the same implementation programme
  as end clients, emerging with the skills and certification to deliver Jube independently
- **Expert of Record** — the Jube developer is available as named technical authority on
  partner-led engagements, supporting governance, audit, and regulatory documentation
  requirements
- **Co-delivery** — for complex or heavily regulated implementations, a Jube developer is
  available to work alongside your team directly
- **Ongoing support** — Enterprise support arrangements are available for clients your team
  has implemented and certified

**Who this is for**

System integrators, technology consultancies, and regional delivery partners serving banks,
fintechs, and payment institutions in emerging and growth markets.

**Getting started**

The natural first step is putting your team through the [Jube training and implementation
programme](/jube-training). From there, certification and a partner relationship follow naturally.
